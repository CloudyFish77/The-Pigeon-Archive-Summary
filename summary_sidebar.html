The Pigeon Archive: Exhibition Summary

This site is a post-exhibition reflection of COO.exe, a pigeon surveillance system disguised as a bird archive. What began with coos and breadcrumbs unfolded into a loop of observation, decoding, and quiet submission. Here, you can revisit the video chapters and the documents once printed from the archive. Click a title to re-enter that segment and view the associated printed material below each video.
